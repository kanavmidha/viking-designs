Start your Project!

Get in Touch

  • Reach out to Viking Design via our contact form, email, or phone.
  • Discuss your initial ideas, goals, and timelines.
  • Schedule a consultation to kick off the project.

Understanding your Vision

  • Conduct an in-depth consultation to understand your brand, target audience, and specific needs.
  • Gather inspiration, references, and any existing materials.
  • Define project objectives, scope, and deliverables.

Project Design and Approval

  • Develop initial design concepts based on the vision and goals.
  • Present wireframes, mockups, and design drafts for your feedback.
  • Revise and refine the design until it meets your approval.

Project Development

  • Turn approved designs into a fully functional website.
  • Implement responsive design, ensuring optimal performance across devices.
  • Integrate necessary features, content management systems, and SEO elements.

Project launch

  • Conduct thorough testing for functionality, performance, and compatibility.
  • Provide a final review and gather your approval for launch.
  • Deploy the website live, ensuring a smooth transition and minimal downtime.

Maintenance (Optional)

  • Offer ongoing maintenance services to keep your website updated and secure.
  • Provide support for updates, bug fixes, and performance enhancements.
  • Regularly review and optimize the site to ensure continued success.
